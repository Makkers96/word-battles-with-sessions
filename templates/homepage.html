Word Battler

How to Play

Use the letters available to come up with a word that would beat the word shown in a fight. Interpret that how you will ;).

If you lose to the word, you lose 1 hp. Reach 0 HP, and it's game over.

If you beat the word, you gain ink. (flat amount based on lvl with +10% interest of total ink)

Beat levels to unlock spells. (Spells cost ink.)

Get as high level as you can!

Good Luck, and Have fun!
